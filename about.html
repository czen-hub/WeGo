Reclaiming the Gig Economy

For too long, the "gig economy" has meant working more for less, with no safety net and no future. We are changing the math.

The "Army Model" of Service

Our mission is to transition the wealth created by technology back into the hands of the people who power it. We operate on three strict principles:

  • Purpose: To provide high-quality, human-led transportation.
  • Commitment: 20 years of dedicated membership.
  • Reward: A guaranteed $2,500/month lifetime pension starting at age 60, backed by a diversified fleet of autonomous assets.

The 10-Year Roadmap

The Asset Moat: By Year 10, we will own 50,000 Autonomous Vehicles and 200 Service Hubs.

Universal Coverage: A national revenue stream of $4.27 Billion ensures independence from venture capital.

The Retirement Trust: A Sovereign Endowment ensuring every member is fully collateralized.

Built for Santa Clara. Owned by You.

Whether you need a safe ride for your family, secure medical logistics, or want to earn a fair wage as a driver-owner, you belong with WeGo.
